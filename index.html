01 — The Theme

What is
The Surge?

Eagles Youth Camp 2026 is built on a powerful vision from John 7:38 — that those who believe in Christ carry within them a surge of living water, streaming from the heart into every area of life.

The Surge is an invitation to open your heart fully to God — streams from the heart, rising as a surge of His presence, overflowing into every area of your life and everyone around you.

04 — FAQ

Your Questions,
answered.

What is Eagles Youth Camp 2026?+

EYC26 is a 4-day residential youth camp organised by The Christian Brethren Youth Ministry. Themed “The Surge — streams from the heart” from John 7:38, it is a time of worship, teaching, fellowship, and spiritual renewal for young people.

Who can attend?+

EYC26 is open to youth and young adults. Members of The Christian Brethren Church are given priority but all are welcome. Only 120 spots are available — register early.

Is registration really free?+

Yes. Registration for EYC26 is completely free. Claim your spot before all 120 are taken — registration is first come, first served.

What should I bring to camp?+

Bible, notebook, pen, comfortable clothing suitable for activities, toiletries, bedding (if required), and an open heart ready to encounter God.

What does “The Surge” mean?+

Based on John 7:38, “The Surge — streams from the heart” speaks to the truth that every believer carries rivers of living water within them. The camp is designed to ignite that surge — letting what God placed in your heart overflow into every life you touch.

Where is Redemption City?+

Redemption City is the venue for EYC26. Full address and travel directions will be sent to all confirmed registrants via email.
